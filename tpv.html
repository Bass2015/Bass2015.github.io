Point Of Sale system.

Making SQL databases user-friendly

I developed a customized Point of Sale (POS) system tailored specifically for a clothes alteration workshop. The primary objective of this project was to create an efficient and streamlined solution for managing customer orders, inventory, and financial transactions within the workshop. To achieve this, I designed and implemented a remote SQL database from scratch, ensuring its compatibility and seamless integration with the POS system. By leveraging my data science skills, I successfully built a robust and user-friendly system that enables the workshop to effectively track and manage orders, optimize inventory management, and maintain accurate financial records.
This project utilizes data science principles to develop practical solutions that meet the unique requirements of specific business domains.
