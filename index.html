About

After my studies in Biology, some travel, and quite some different jobs, I jumped on that fast moving train we call "programming". I soon noticed that it had so many wagons that I would never have time to explore all of them in detail. Should I visit the game development wagon? Or the algorithms one? What about Artificial Inteligence? Wait, what? Can computers have neural networks?

The choice was hard, but what I want the most is to use my time and energy to add value to the world. In this data-driven environment where we live, Data Science and Analysis was the obvious choice.

So here I am, with an official academic course on App Development, two Data Science bootcamps, and 7 languages, to tackle any problem that I can find.
