Algorithm for Heart Rate detection

Evaluating different methods for PPG signal processing

I developed a custom Grid Search algorithm designed specifically for filtering and processing PPG signals. The primary objective of this algorithm is to accurately extract HR and HRV values. The algorithm explores various parameter combinations and computes the error between the true values, obtained from labeled ECG signals, and the estimated values, derived from raw PPG signals. I also created a custom application that simplifies the labeling of ECG signals associated with PPG signals, a demo video is showed above.
My team and I are working on this ongoing project, in collaboration with Brno University in the Czech Republic.
